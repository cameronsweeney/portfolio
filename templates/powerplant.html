Power Plant

Red and Blue

The Power Plant is a small dungeon just below Route 10. In addition to random wild encounters while walking inside, the player can find Zapdos hiding in the deepest corner and several Voltorb and Electrode disguised as Poké Balls.

Fixed encounters

Zapdos

Zapdos is located in the upper left corner of the map. You will have to go the long way through most of the Power Plant to make it there, but there is a one-way exit left of Zapdos. It will bring you to the side of the building outside, letting you skip any wild encounters on the way back. Zapdos appears at Lv. 50 with the moves ThunderShock and Drill Peck.

Voltorb, Electrode

Voltorb will appear in random walking encounters, but there are also many Voltorb and Electrode disguised as items/Poké Balls in the overworld. There are six Voltorb and two Electrode (refer to the map for exact locations).
